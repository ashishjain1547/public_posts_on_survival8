1. Boil water (for 1 cup Macroni 5 cups water), add Bambino Macaroni, 1 tsp salt and 1 tsp oil. After Macaroni is cooked, remove from flame strain and run cold water through it and keep aside. 

2. Heat oil (3 tsp) in a pan, and add jeera (3 tsp) and fry well. Then add the chopped onions (1 no.) and saute for 1 minute and add the beans (100g cut into long piece diagonally). Carrots (100g cut into thin long pieces) and fry for 1/2 minute.

3. After that add Bambino Macaroni, Onions (1 no. chopped), tomato puree (100g), chat masala (3 tsp) and mix well and remove from flame, after 1 minute, mix 1 tsp of lime juice, garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot.

Above given ingredients are for 225g Macaroni.
